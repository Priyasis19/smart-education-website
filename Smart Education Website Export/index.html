Joining is simple.

There is no online form: our office creates every student account personally, so nothing can go wrong.

01

Call or visit us

Phone us or come to the institute. Tell us the student’s name, class, and which course you are interested in.

02

We create your account

Our office registers the student and hands you a slip with a Student ID and password. Keep it safe.

03

Log in and start

Open the Portal from the menu, type the ID and password from your slip, and see your classes, homework, and fees.
